Balloons and Static Electricity

Scene Summary

Simulation contains a Play Area and a Control Panel. The Play Area is a small room. The Control Panel has buttons and switches to change conditions in the room.

  • Currently, room has a balloon, a sweater, and a removable wall.
  • Yellow balloon, in center of play area, evenly between sweater and wall. Sweater is at far left, wall at far right.
  • Balloon has a few pairs of negative and positive charges. The sweater and wall have many pairs of negative and positive charges. All have neutral net charge.

Grab balloon to play.
